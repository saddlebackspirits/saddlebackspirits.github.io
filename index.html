Whiskey Education

Whisk(e)y is our passion, and the stories about distilleries, production, blending, and the characters and flavors involved at every step of the process are what give this spirit its unique character. We can walk you through the ins and outs at any level from beginner to expert:

  • What's the difference between whiskey and whisky?
  • Is bourbon the only American whiskey?
  • Why are certain years of Clynelish, Bowmore, or Glendronach so much better than others?

And if you come up with questions we can't immediately answer, we love to do the research!

Menu Curation

One of the main functions of our consultancy is to assist with the curation and sourcing of world-class whiskey menus.

An impressive wine list has always been a hallmark of a high-quality restaurant, but customers are increasingly discerning in other areas as well. If your list consists of whatever your local distributor had on hand, you are leaving money and customer goodwill on the table.

We can help assemble a whiskey list (and other spirits as well) that will get the attention of even the most discerning connoisseur, whether you keep 5 or 500 bottles in stock.

Tasting Events

Remote or in-person tasting events are the best way to get to know whiskey. Our primary tasting coordinator has conducted many such events and is happy to cater to multiple levels of expertise. From restaurant staff seminars to office parties, we can guide you through a deep or broad cross-section of whiskies and answer whatever questions you might have.

Important Note: Whiskey is a powerful spirit and safety is always our primary concern, so please take this into consideration when planning your event.

Contact Us

We are based in Dallas, Texas, but make regular visits to New York, Colorado, Montana, California, and the UK, so are often able to accommodate face-to-face meetings in any of these locales. If your project demands more urgent interaction, we are happy to schedule remote consultations and coordinate joint live tasting sessions or make custom travel arrangements as necessary.

About Saddleback

We are a loose collective of whisk(e)y enthusiasts excited to share our experience with those looking to expand their palates and mindsets.
